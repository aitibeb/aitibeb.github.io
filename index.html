🇪🇹 Making AI Work in Ethiopia

Language Gap

Limited support for Amharic and 80+ local languages in global AI systems.

Data Scarcity

Lack of structured, open, and culturally relevant datasets for training.

Accessibility

Need for simple, mobile-first, low-bandwidth, and affordable AI tools.

Infrastructure

Connectivity gaps and computing limitations that affect real-world adoption.
