🇪🇹 Making AI Work in Ethiopia

AI በኢትዮጵያ እንዲሰራ የምናደርገው እንዴት ነው?
We're tackling real barriers with community-driven solutions.

🗣️

Language Gap

Most AI tools don't understand Amharic or Ethiopia's 80+ local languages.

Our Approach:
  • Train models on Ethiopian text & speech
  • Community-driven translation
  • Open-source language resources
📦

Data Scarcity

Limited public datasets in local languages slow down AI development.

Our Approach:
  • Ethiopian Dataset Hub (open & curated)
  • Tools for community data collection
  • Ethical data sharing guidelines
📱

Accessibility

Low-end phones, limited data, and intermittent connectivity.

Our Approach:
  • Offline-first tool design
  • Lightweight models (<5MB)
  • SMS/USSD fallback options
⚡

Infrastructure

Limited computing resources and cloud access hinder local AI innovation.

Our Approach:
  • Browser-based training (no GPU needed)
  • Partnerships with local tech hubs
  • Edge-AI optimization guides
