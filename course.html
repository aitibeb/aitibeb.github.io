Understanding Machine Learning

AI doesn't "know" things like humans do. Instead, it learns by finding patterns in data. Think of it like teaching a child: you show many examples, they notice similarities, and gradually make predictions.

🔑 Key Concept: Training Data → Pattern Recognition → Prediction

🇪🇹 Ethiopian Example

Imagine teaching someone to recognize እንጀራ (Injera). You don't give them a dictionary definition. You show them photos, textures, and even smells. After enough examples, they can identify it anywhere. AI works the same way — but with numbers instead of senses.

  • 📌 AI learns from examples, not rules
  • 📌 More diverse data = better predictions
  • 📌 Ethiopian AI needs Ethiopian data
