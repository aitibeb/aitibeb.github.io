We believe artificial intelligence should empower everyone — not just those who speak English or have high-speed internet. AI TiBeB exists to close the language gap, amplify local knowledge, and ensure Ethiopia shapes its own AI future.

Our Story 📖

From a university project to a national movement — here's how AI TiBeB began.

2023

🌱 The Idea

Three students at Addis Ababa University noticed AI tools failed to understand Amharic. They asked: "What if AI learned our languages first?"

2024

🤝 First Partners

Collaborated with iCog Labs, Ethiopian AI Association, and 5 regional universities to collect the first open Amharic dataset.

2025

🚀 Public Launch

AI TiBeB went live with 3 courses, 2 tools, and 1,000+ community members. First Oromo and Tigrinya resources released.

2026

🌍 Scaling Impact

Now supporting 12 Ethiopian languages, 50+ open datasets, and partnerships across East Africa. The journey continues.

Our Ethical Commitments ✨

Technology without ethics is just code. Here's how we build responsibly.

🔒

Data Sovereignty

Ethiopian data stays in Ethiopia. We never sell, lease, or exploit community-contributed data.

🌍

Community First

Local speakers validate all translations and models. AI serves people — not the reverse.

♿

Accessibility by Design

Every tool works offline, on low-end phones, and with minimal data. No one left behind.

🔍

Transparency

Open models, open data, open decisions. We publish our methodology, funding, and impact reports.

Read our full Ethical AI Framework (PDF) or ask us anything.
