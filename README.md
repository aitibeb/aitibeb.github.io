AI TiBeB 🌍🤖

Making AI accessible and usable in local languages

---
🚀 About the Project

AI TiBeB is an initiative focused on advancing **inclusive artificial intelligence** by supporting the development, accessibility, and usability of AI in **local languages across Africa**.

While AI technologies are rapidly evolving, many African languages remain underrepresented in datasets, tools, and applications. AI TiBeB aims to bridge this gap by enabling language-inclusive AI systems that are relevant, accessible, and impactful.

---
🎯 Objectives
- Promote AI development in **local African languages**
- Improve access to **datasets and language resources**
- Support **developers, researchers, and innovators**
- Encourage **open and collaborative AI ecosystems**
- Enable **real-world applications** in education, agriculture, health, and governance

🌍 Why It Matters

AI systems are only as good as the data and contexts they are built on. Without local language support, large populations are excluded from the benefits of AI.

AI TiBeB contributes to:

- Digital inclusion
- Linguistic diversity in AI
- Locally relevant innovation
